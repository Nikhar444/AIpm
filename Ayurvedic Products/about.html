Our Story

Ancient wisdom for modern beauty

From Tradition to Innovation

Founded in 2018 by Ayurvedic practitioner Maya Sharma, Ayurveda Beauty was born from a passion to bring the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda to modern skincare routines.

After studying traditional Ayurvedic formulations in Kerala, India, Maya worked with botanists and cosmetic chemists to create products that honor ancient recipes while meeting the needs of today's consumers.

Our mission is simple: to create effective, natural skincare products that work in harmony with your unique constitution, bringing balance and radiance to your skin.

Maya Sharma, Founder

Our Ingredients

We use only the finest Ayurvedic herbs and botanicals, many of which have been used for thousands of years for their medicinal and cosmetic properties.

Kumkuma (Saffron)

Known as "golden spice," saffron brightens the complexion and reduces hyperpigmentation.

Good for: Vata & Pitta

Nimba (Neem)

A powerful purifier that cleanses and detoxifies the skin while fighting blemishes.

Good for: Pitta & Kapha

Ashwagandha

An adaptogenic herb that helps skin resist stressors and signs of aging.

Good for: All Doshas
